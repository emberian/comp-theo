## Visual

The poster is a dense, nine-panel infographic with an ornate sci-fi manuscript layout, using gold serif headings, cyan technical labels, and orange emphasis on a dark gridded cosmic background. It combines symbolic diagrams of prediction, learning loops, blowtorch-like formation, scales of justice, orbital systems, neural networks, and timeline icons to contrast self-prediction with moral evaluation. The composition reads like a theological systems map: prediction and structure are visually mechanized, while justice is repeatedly marked as an extra evaluative layer that does not arise automatically.
