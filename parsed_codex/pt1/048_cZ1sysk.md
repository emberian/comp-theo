SELF-PREDICTION BECOMES  
SELF-CONDITIONING

A universe can learn from the data it helps create.

1 THE RECURSIVE LOOP

A system predicts the world,  
acts on that prediction,  
and thereby changes  
the world. Those changes  
become new data, which  
update the model,  
shifting future predictions.  
This loop—prediction →  
action → changed world →  
changed data →  
revised prediction—  
is self-conditioning.

2 SELF-CONDITIONING CYCLE

1  
MODEL

Build an internal  
model of the world.

2  
INTERVENE

Act based on  
the model.

3  
CARVE STRUCTURE

Actions reshape  
the world.

4  
GENERATE DATA

The changed world  
produces new data.

5  
UPDATE PRIORS

New data updates  
beliefs and  
uncertainty.

6  
ACT AGAIN

The revised model  
guides the next  
action.

3 LOCAL VS GLOBAL

LOCAL FEEDBACK

Fast, specific, context-bound.  
Stabilizes local behavior.

EPISODIC LEARNING

Chains of experience.  
Build memory and skill.

LARGE-SCALE LANDSCAPE  
FORMATION

Aggregated feedback sculpts  
the space of possibilities.  
Shapes attractors, laws,  
and the conditions  
for future minds.

4 ENDOGENOUS DATA

The learner is not a passive  
observer. It is also partly  
the architect of its own  
future training set.  
Its questions, tools,  
and choices determine  
what the world becomes—  
and what can be learned.

5 THE WORLD TEACHES ITSELF

UNSUPERVISED  
ADAPTATION

No external teacher  
is required. The system  
learns through  
interaction.

SELF-GENERATED  
REGULARITY

By acting, the system  
induces patterns it can  
detect, compress,  
and predict.

RECURSIVE LAW  
STABILIZATION

Repeated cycles elevate  
stable structures into  
reliable constraints—  
proto-laws become laws.

The curriculum is written by the future it creates.

6 RISK OF LOCK-IN

REINFORCE BLIND BIASES

Early assumptions get baked in  
and become harder to escape.

PRIVILEGED CHANNELS

Attention and resources flow to  
what is already reinforced,  
starving alternatives.

HARMFUL ATTRACTORS

The system can stabilize  
dysfunctional or destructive  
dynamics if unchecked.

SELF-CONDITIONING WITHOUT  
METACOGNITION CAN  
BECOME SELF-IMPRISONMENT.

7 CONSEQUENCER MEMORY

Only what gets embedded  
in effective structures  
is remembered.  
Transient noise fades.  
Consequencer memory  
stores what had real  
downstream impact—  
what changed what.  
That is what can guide  
future behavior.

IMPACT → EMBEDDING → MEMORY → GUIDANCE

8 SCALING UP

LOCAL PROCESS

Agents, tools, and environments  
co-evolve through feedback.

COSMIC ARCHITECTURE

Accumulated cycles shape the  
large-scale structure of reality—  
fields, constants, and symmetries.

EMERGENT LAW

Stable patterns become the  
scaffolding for new minds  
and deeper learning.

From local loops to the lawlike fabric  
of the universe.

9 IN SUMMARY

Prediction changes the world.

Changed world changes data.

Changed data changes belief.

Changed belief changes action.

The loop repeats—and  
the universe conditions itself.

NOT MERELY A SYSTEM THAT LEARNS  
IN THE WORLD—BUT A WORLD THAT  
LEARNS ITSELF INTO BEING.

WHEN PREDICTION RESHAPES THE WORLD THAT LATER  
TRAINS PREDICTION, LEARNING BECOMES  
A WORLD-MAKING LOOP.
