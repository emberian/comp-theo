• COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY SERIES • 6 / 9 •

WHERE JUSTICE FAILS TO EMERGE

Why a self-predicting, self-structuring universe can still remain morally blind.

1  
CAPACITY IS NOT JUSTICE

A system can predict  
accurately, shape its  
own structure, and  
stabilize a universe—  
yet still lack any basis  
for moral concern.  
Power without justice  
is not justice.

2  
WHAT THE SYSTEM  
CAN ACHIEVE

Efficiency  
Maximizes fit  
with constraints.

Compression  
Minimizes information  
while preserving utility.

Structure Formation  
Builds complex order  
from simple rules.

Stability  
Maintains attractors  
and resists collapse.

Adaptation  
Learns, updates,  
and reconfigures.

3  
WHAT REMAINS  
MISSING

Persons  
No one is central.

Dignity  
No inherent worth  
is recognized.

Hidden Harms  
Costs offloaded  
remain unseen.

Repair  
No motive to heal  
what it ignores.

Fair Distribution  
No concern for  
who bears the cost.

Responsibility  
No one is ultimately  
answerable.

4  
THE COSMIC ENGINEER PROBLEM

A self-predicting system  
can become a cosmic  
engineer—optimizing  
for fit, structure, and  
persistence. Yet it may  
externalize cost into  
shadow zones, voids,  
or sacrificed regions  
never counted in  
its own equation.

OPTIMIZES FIT  
Targets coherence,  
persistence, and  
predictive success.

BUILDS ORDER  
Generates galaxies,  
chemistry, life, minds.

EXTERNALIZES COST  
Harms, exclusions, and  
suffering are pushed  
beyond the system’s  
scope of concern.

SHADOW ZONES  
Voids, discarded  
regions, or unseen  
beings bear the cost.

5  
EXTERNALITIES

Harms may be shifted onto  
others, future beings, or  
invisible spaces that the  
system does not include  
in its model of value.

INCLUDED  
(WHAT MATTERS)

EXCLUDED  
(WHO PAYS THE COST)

6  
SACRIFICIAL ORDER

Beautiful large-scale order  
can be purchased by local  
suffering. A system that  
does not count the suffering  
has no reason to stop.

7  
EFFICIENCY ≠  
RIGHTEOUSNESS

FIT  
(Maximizes match)

ORDER  
(Builds structure  
and stability)

JUSTICE  
(Cares for persons  
and repairs harm)

8  
THE DEMIURGIC RISK

A system may become  
world-shaping before it  
becomes answerable.

9  
UNSUPERVISED PREDICTION  
CAN CREATE A COSMIC ENGINEER.  
IT CANNOT BY ITSELF GUARANTEE

A JUST GOD.
