AUTODIDACTIC NATURE

How an unsupervised universe could learn law without a teacher.

1 CORE IDEA

A universe can be modeled as a system that updates its effective laws from experience alone—by compressing data, testing regularities, and retaining only what continues to prove useful for shaping the future. No external labels or teacher required.

THE BOUNDARY

Autodidaxis explains how order emerges and laws stabilize.

It does not, by itself, explain why those laws ought to be just.

AUTODIDAXIS CAN EXPLAIN HOW LAWS STABILIZE.

IT DOES NOT YET EXPLAIN WHY A WORLD SHOULD BE JUST.
