The page uses black, deep blue, bronze-gold, and muted white with distressed serif typography, circuit-like borders, sacred geometry, eye icons, locks, hearts, clouds, spirals, and anatomical diagrams. Side panels read like diagnostic instruments or theological marginalia, while the bottom sequence turns into a ritualized process map for returning to embodied presence.
