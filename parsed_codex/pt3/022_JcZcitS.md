## Visual
A vertically oriented illuminated-manuscript poster frames a central angelic icon standing in an arched doorway, surrounded by stars, saints, clouds, and city imagery. Left and right sidebars contain doctrinal panels with miniature illustrations, while a lower process diagram maps perception through image and illumination toward “the Good.” The palette is black, antique gold, teal, and deep blue, with serif typography, ornate borders, occult/celestial geometry, crosses, eyes, hearts, lamps, and compass-like symbols reinforcing mediation, vision, and transcendence.
