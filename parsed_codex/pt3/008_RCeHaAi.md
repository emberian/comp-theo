A tall, ornate esoteric poster is split into a dark red left “capture” path and a gold-lit right “liberation” path around a meditating central figure with glowing head and heart. The design uses framed panels, arrows, circular diagrams, sigils, lotus and eye symbols, and a practice-loop flowchart to turn attention training into a ritualized exorcism map. The palette is black, ember red, and antique gold, with engraved occult-manuscript typography and dense decorative linework.
