## Visual

The poster is a dense black-and-gold esoteric infographic arranged in framed panels around a central synapse, reward-cycle triad, and recovery pathway. It combines neuroscience diagrams, a brain reward circuit, receptor gates, body silhouettes, an ouroboros-like circular symbol, a meditating figure, and alchemical geometry to frame addiction as learned system dynamics. The style uses antique serif typography, distressed parchment-like texture, fine-line ornament, and accent colors of purple, teal, and amber to distinguish circuits, cycles, and stages.
