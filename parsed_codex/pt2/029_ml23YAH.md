## Visual

The poster is an eight-panel grid with numbered modules, heavy black backgrounds, thin white borders, condensed uppercase headings, and glowing yellow, orange, red, cyan, and green accent text. Each panel uses a different explanatory diagram: a state-space landscape, a preorder funnel, a causal cycle, a red suffering spiral, a world map of captured and externalized flows, a cosmic feedback process, a justice redesign flowchart, and a golden branching tree. Arrows, checkmarks, x-marks, upward arrows, and looping paths carry extra meaning by marking optimization, exclusion, feedback, and transformation.
