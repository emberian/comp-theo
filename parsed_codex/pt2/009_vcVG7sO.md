## Visual

The poster uses an illuminated-manuscript layout with ornate borders, cream parchment texture, navy-and-gold palette, and serif/small-caps typography. Seven numbered panels combine medieval religious imagery with systems diagrams: mirror selfhood, body-model nodes, a timeline and scorecard, pain as a flowchart, self-preservation as a shield/tree emblem, shame/guilt/inadequacy figures, and a coordinating hub network. The bottom formula panel and final aphorism frame the essay’s thesis as both mathematical relation and theological-style maxim.
