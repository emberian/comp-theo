## Visual

An ornate dark-gold and blue mystical poster arranges a central wounded human figure inside a cathedral-like archive of diagrams, with glowing red pain patterns transforming into blue stored memory. A horizontal process diagram runs across the middle from error to world reshaping, while side panels contrast unconserved suffering with consecrated persistence. The style mixes illuminated manuscript borders, occult geometry, computational flowchart symbols, and serif capitals in a sacred-technical typographic system.
