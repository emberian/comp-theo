## Visual

An ornate dark-blue-and-gold poster is arranged like an illuminated occult/scientific manuscript, with a monumental temple at center surrounded by sidebars, bottom diagram panels, constellations, circuitry, and sacred-geometry frames. The imagery combines a robed initiate, temple architecture, network diagrams, matrix notation, physics formulas, an ouroboros, scales, and error-to-world flowcharts to imply a self-teaching cosmos. Typography is mostly gold engraved serif lettering with cyan and magenta diagram accents, giving the panel an alchemical, theological, and computational mood.
