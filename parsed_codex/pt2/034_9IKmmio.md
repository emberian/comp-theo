## Visual
A dense occult-scientific poster arranged as a triptych: explanatory side columns flank a central cosmic machine or observatory made of rings, towers, light beams, and circuitry-like paths. The diagrams use alchemical, astronomical, and network symbols to frame “criteria” as self-forming evaluative structures rather than externally supplied rules. The palette is black, deep blue, gold, and occasional magenta/cyan, with engraved serif typography and fine ornamental borders evoking a mystical technical manuscript.
