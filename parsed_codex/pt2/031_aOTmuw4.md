## Visual
A dark, illuminated poster combines a human neural silhouette, branching river landscapes, storm and bridge scenes, and two diagrammatic loop panels to contrast unskillful and skillful responses to frustration. The palette uses deep black-blue backgrounds with gold serif typography, teal for adaptive/flourishing paths, and orange for torment/amplification. Icons such as mismatch clouds, blockage cubes, eyes, shields, bridges, and circular arrows carry the argument visually as process symbols for expectation, attention, regulation, and repeated loops.
