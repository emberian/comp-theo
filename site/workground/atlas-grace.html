Atlas — Grace across every instrument

Under lab4's function-approximation agent, grace — an exogenous edge that rescues a stuck agent — was significant at +0.10. The robustness atlas re-tests that single result across the full instrument grid: three agents (myopic, tabular, linear FA) × three worlds (single-basin, two-basin, community-found). Does "an exogenous edge rescues you" generalise, or was it FA-specific?

The ladder. lab4 found grace significant under one instrument (FA, detected single basin). This page is the atlas slice: same mechanism, every instrument, one factorial each. Honest verdict

Grace is robust in 4 of 9 cells: myopic·single (+0.143), myopic·community (+0.065), tabular·single (+0.033), and tabular·community (+0.034). In every robust cell the sign is positive — where grace does anything, an exogenous rescue edge helps.

lab4's headline does not generalise to its own instrument. lab4 reported grace at +0.10 under the function-approximation agent. Across this atlas the entire fa row is null or slightly negative: fa·single −0.007 (CI spans 0), fa·two-basin exactly 0, fa·community +0.001 (CI spans 0). None is robust. The +0.10 lab4 result is not reproduced by lab5kit's FA configuration; the effect lab4 attributed to grace appears to be instrument-specific to that earlier setup, not a property of grace as a mechanism. This is a negative result and is reported as one.

The two-basin world annihilates grace entirely. Every agent scores exactly 0.000 with a degenerate [0, 0] CI in two_basin, alongside the lowest detector Jaccard (≈0.04–0.08). When the basin detector fails to localise the trap, the rescue edge has nothing coherent to bypass and the mechanism is inert across all three agents.

Where grace generalises: the simpler agents. The pattern is the reverse of an FA story — grace earns its keep for the myopic and tabular agents (which lack a lossy spectral map and can get genuinely stuck), and contributes nothing for the FA agent, whose function approximation already smooths over the trap that grace would otherwise rescue. "An exogenous edge rescues you" generalises across worlds for the unaided agents (single and community both robust), but it does not generalise across agents: it is real for myopic/tabular, absent for FA.
