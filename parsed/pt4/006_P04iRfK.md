## Visual
A dark, richly gilded devotional-style poster with an ornate border and vertical side panels of small captioned roundel icons (a book, scales, a doorway, an ascending figure, open hands). The center shows a starry night sky with a crescent moon, a radiant hand reaching down from a burst of light, pouring a luminous stream onto a vast open ledger-book whose pages are split by a glowing chasm of light leading to a distant horizon. The palette is deep black-blue and gold with strong divine-light imagery; the lower edge has small landscape vignettes including a barren tree and a sunrise over kneeling figures. A footer banner reads the series title flanked by cross motifs.
