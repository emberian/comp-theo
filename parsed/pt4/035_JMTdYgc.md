## Visual
A dark, densely gilded liturgical poster organized into three horizontal "layers." The top row shows six numbered ritual stages (Gather, Confess, Listen, Offer, Receive, Return), each with a small glowing scene of robed figures around an altar. The middle "Layer Two" band shows network/architecture vignettes; "Layer Three" presents five protocol panels with a glowing open book, lists, and an eye motif. Side margins hold cartouches for Truth, Humility, Mercy, Return. Palette is black, antique gold, ember-orange, and teal with ornate manuscript framing and heraldic corner roundels.
