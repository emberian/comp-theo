REVENGE AS FALSE ACCOUNTING

mirrored harm
You strike back and see your own wound reflected.

the counterfeit balance
It weighs equal, yet the scales are broken.

counter-pain is not repair
Matching the blow does not mend the rest.

what vengeance cannot restore
The dead do not return. The past does not unbreak.

the wound replicated
You copy the injury and call it justice. It is only inheritance.

Revenge promises equilibrium, but it often
multiplies the injury-field rather than
repairing the thing that was broken.

THE LEDGER OF THE WOUND

NOTES FROM THE MARGIN

REMEMBER
