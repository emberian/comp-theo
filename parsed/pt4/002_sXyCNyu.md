EVENT BECOMES IDENTITY
What was done or done to you is recorded.
What is recorded is repeated.
What is repeated becomes who you are.

THE INWARD TRIBUNAL
No witness is needed.
The court moves within.
The verdict is constant.

SELF-ACCUSATION
The accuser becomes the one accused.
The sentence requires no judge.

DEBT CARRIED AS SELFHOOD
A wound unattended is a debt.
A debt internalized is a self.

TRUTH VS. CONDEMNATION
Truth names what is real.
Condemnation names what must be condemned.
One heals.
The other imprisons.
