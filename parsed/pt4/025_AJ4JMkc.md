## Visual
A dark occult-diagram poster in gold and crimson line art. At top center a glowing teal icosahedron bearing a Σ sits enthroned beneath a crown, flanked by carved statue figures, representing the deified proxy/metric. Three labeled circular nodes ("The Soul" with a kneeling supplicant figure, "The Institution" with grade/profit/ranking charts, "The Model" with a neural network graph) are connected by dotted flow lines along a U-shaped arc. Side margins list definitions and attribute lists, with corner roundels (a zodiac-like ram, a flame sigil) and an arched sacred-architecture frame.
