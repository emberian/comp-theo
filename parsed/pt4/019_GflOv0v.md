VERITAS · BONUM · PULCHRUM

GRACE DOES NOT CANCEL FORMATION

VII OF IX

A COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY CARD FOR THE PILGRIM OF PRACTICED GRACE

GRACE IS A GIFT
Unmerited.
Freely given.
Always free.

CALLED TO COOPERATE
You do not earn
grace, but you
can embrace
its work.

NOT AUTOMATIC
It does not
grow by itself.
It must be
rewarded.

DAILY PRACTICE
Small fidelities
build deep
formation.

GIFT MUST BE PRACTICED

RECEIVED AND CULTIVATED

THE SEED MUST BE TENDED

FORMATION TAKES TIME

YOU ARE A GARDEN
Soul, seed, water,
light, and time
work together
in unity.

ROOTED IN CHRIST
Apart from Him
you can do
nothing. In Him,
you bear fruit.

SEASONS OF FORMATION

PLANTED IN FAITH

WATERED IN OBEDIENCE

WINTER

THE SEED HOLDS LIFE

SPRING

ROOTED IN HOPE

GROWN IN LOVE

BEARING FRUIT

AUTUMN

THE SOIL
[illegible] habits,
hidden choices,
silent
suffering.

THE WATER
Prayer.
Devotion.
Repentance.
Service.

NO SHORTCUTS, ONLY SECRETS,
SLOWER THINGS, LASTING FRUIT.

GLORY IS A HARVEST
What is formed
in hidden faith
will be revealed
in fullness.

FOR THE WORLD
Formation is
not for you alone,
but for their
good.

GRACE BEGINS THE WORK.
FORMATION CARRIES IT THROUGH.
GLORY COMPLETES IT.

INVOCATION
Lord, plant what You promise.
Tend in me to yield what You have planted.
Form in me the bloom of Your love.

SEALED IN FAITH

RELEASED IN GRACE

TRUTH WITHOUT EVASION

HOPE WITHOUT ILLUSION

COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY

## Visual
A dark, gilded engraving-style poster with an ornate border and corner Roman-numeral/cross medallions. The central image shows a radiant hand pouring from a watering can, with a beam of divine light and a glowing dove/sun above, nurturing a small sprout that grows from rich dark soil whose roots spread into a circular "SEASONS OF FORMATION" wheel diagram with seasonal labels. Vertical side columns hold circular emblem icons (gift box, hourglass, garden, harvest, sun) with captioned aphorisms, flanked by two open illustrated books (THE SOIL / THE WATER). A lower band has small narrative vignettes and an invocation banner above the footer series title, in warm gold and deep brown-black tones.
