## Visual
A dark, gilded engraving-style poster with an ornate border and corner cross/Roman-numeral medallions. The center shows a radiant hand descending from a starry sky pouring a stream of golden light into a chalice held by another hand below, above a foreground of stone ledger-tablets ("MERIT-WAGE CHART") scattered amid ruins, contrasting accounting with grace. Vertical side columns hold circular emblem icons (gift box, crown, scales, throne, sunburst) with captioned aphorisms; four small banner labels list grace phrases beside the central scene. A lower band displays six labeled step icons (INITIATED BY LOVE, GIFT GIVEN, RECEIVED BY FAITH, TRANSFORM THE SOUL, BEARS FRUIT IN FREEDOM, GLORY RETURNS) with a lamb emblem and footer banners, in warm gold and deep brown-black tones.
