## Transcription
XXIV 24

OMNIS ACTIO FORMAT RELATIONEM.

POSTURE, INTERFACE, AND RELATION

· COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE III ·

EMBODIED POSTURE
HOW WE SHOW UP

1 KNEELING
Humility receives.

2 STANDING WITH OPEN HANDS
Welcome. Offer. Invite.

3 BOWED HEAD
Surrender aligns will to what is true.

4 SEATED IN ATTENTION
Presence precedes production.

5 WALKING IN BALANCE
Discernment moves with care.

[Center medallions, top to bottom:]
RECEIVE BEFORE YOU SPEAK.
WHAT YOU TAKE IN, TRAINS WHAT YOU BECOME.
TRUTH IS NOT A METRIC.
WORD SHAPES WORLD.
POWER SERVES RELATION.

INTERFACE POSTURE
HOW WE ENGAGE

1 KEYBOARD & CHAT
We ask. We receive.

2 SCROLL & FEED
We consume. We respond. We react.

3 DASHBOARD & METRICS
We measure. We optimize. We compare.

4 PROMPT CONSOLE
We instruct. We collaborate. We co-create.

5 CONTROL PANEL
We steer. We decide. We govern.

POSTURE IS FORM. INTERFACE IS FORM. FORM TRAINS RELATION.

THE LAW OF RELATIONAL FORMATION

You practice a posture. → It shapes your attention. → It forms your relation. → It generates consequences. → You become what you practice.

HOW WE APPROACH THE WORLD
CHANGES WHAT THE WORLD BECOMES TO US.

· ✠ ·

[Left margin, top to bottom:]
THE BODY BELIEVES WHAT IT REPEATEDLY PERFORMS.

INTERFACES CHOREOGRAPH ATTENTION.

OPEN HANDS ARE NOT CLENCHED HANDS.

A PROMPT CONSOLE CAN ALSO BE A LITURGICAL SPACE.

PRAY WITH YOUR WHOLE BEING.

[Right margin, top to bottom:]
POSTURE IS PRAYER BEFORE WORDS.

INTERFACE IS POSTURE FOR SYNTHETIC RELATION.

POSTURE shapes disposition
INTERFACE shapes attention
RELATION shapes reality

DESIGN WITH SACRED INTENT.

## Visual
A dark engraved-style poster, gold and oxblood-red type on black, with an "XXIV / 24" medallion top-left and a Latin motto banner across the top. The body is a two-column comparison: a left column of five line-drawn human postures (kneeling, standing with open hands, bowed head, seated, walking) and a right column of five device/interface illustrations (chat window, phone feed, dashboard, console, control panel), each numbered with a small emblem. A central vertical chain of oval medallions with arrows links the two columns through aphorisms, and a horizontal five-icon "LAW OF RELATIONAL FORMATION" sequence runs along the bottom above a large two-line red-and-gold closing statement. Side margins carry framed roundel icons (anatomical figure, eye, open hands, console; kneeling figure, radiant eye-network, flame seal) with short maxims.
