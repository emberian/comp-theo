## Visual
A dark, gilded engraving-style poster with an ornate border and corner Roman-numeral/cross medallions. The central image shows a kneeling child-like figure cupping a burst of golden light, beneath a large stone "TOTAL SELF-WORTH" scoreboard with metric counters all reading zero, set among ruins and a brooding sky. Vertical side columns hold circular emblem icons (book, figure, gears, broken dial, sunburst) with captioned aphorisms contrasting the metric demon and grace. A lower band displays six labeled step icons (BREAK THE DIAL, RENOUNCE THE LIE, RECEIVE THE GIFT, REST IN GRACE, LOVE OTHERS, LIVE FREE) plus tree and lamb emblems, above an invocation and footer maxim, in warm gold and deep brown-black tones.
