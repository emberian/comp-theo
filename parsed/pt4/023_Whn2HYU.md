BEYOND THE LEDGER · BEYOND THE LIMIT · BEYOND THE DUE

GRACE EXCEEDS BALANCE.
Love does not
calculate; its
own cost.

MORE THAN BALANCE.
Not fair.
Not owed.
Freely given.

ABUNDANCE AFTER REPAIR.
Restoration is
not the end—
it is the
opening.

OVERFLOW.
Grace spills
into beauty,
joy, wonder,
and delight.

FEAST BEYOND THE LEDGER.
What cannot
be earned
is worthy
of wonder.

FEAST BEYOND THE LEDGER.
The table is
not for all—
and still
it overflows.

OVERFLOW WITHOUT CEASING.
What is given
in grace cannot
be contained
by rules.

ABUNDANCE AFTER REPAIR.
What was lost
is now amply
replaced—
it is multiplied.
