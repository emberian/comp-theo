DO NOT ASK ONLY WHETHER IT WORKS. ASK WHAT WORLD IT TRAINS.

1. WHAT DOES THIS MAKE SACRED?
Does it elevate truth, life, beauty, justice, wisdom, or the common good? Or does it enthrone power, comfort, novelty, or approval?

2. WHAT DOES IT SHAME?
Does it cast out what is weak, inconvenient, or unseen? Does it ridicule humility, grief, doubt, or moral restraint?

3. WHAT DOES IT REWARD?
Does it train for depth, fidelity, courage, and care? Or speed, outrage, flattery, dependence, and performative certainty?

4. WHAT FUTURE DOES IT NORMALIZE?
Does it open possibility, plurality, and responsibility? Or does it narrow imagination, erase alternatives, and harden inevitability?
