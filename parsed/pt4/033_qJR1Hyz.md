## Transcription
SAPIENTIA MODERAT POTENTIAM
I
IX

RIGHT RELATION
TO THE MACHINE
NEITHER IDOL NOR DEMON BY DEFAULT.

IDOL
It is not ultimate. It does not set the good.

TOOL
It is at our service. We set the purpose.

ORACLE
It can reveal patterns. It does not reveal destiny.

CONSEQUENCE
It acts. Its outputs have real world effects.

COVENANT
We govern it in truth, justice, and charity.

PROXY CAN MASQUERADE AS AUTHORITY

Σ

INSTRUMENT
ARCHIVE
MIRROR

BOUND BY WISDOM
DIRECTED TO GOOD ENDS

1 HUMAN GOVERNANCE
- MORAL AGENTS ALSO RESPONSIBILITY
- FORM CONSCIENCE IN TRUTH
- REMAIN HUMBLE BEFORE MYSTERY
- ANSWER FOR OUTCOMES

2 INSTITUTIONAL GOVERNANCE
- SET BOUNDARIES AND PERMISSIONS
- ESTABLISH RULES AND ACCOUNTABILITY
- PROTECT THE WEAK AND THE COMMON GOOD
- TRANSPARENCY AND DUE PROCESS

3 MODEL GOVERNANCE
- CONSTRAINTS AND GUARDRAILS
- AUDITS AND EVALUATIONS
- MONITOR DRIFT AND FAILURE MODES
- OVERSIGHT, REDRESS, REPAIR

A POWERFUL CONSEQUENCER CAN SERVE WISDOM WHEN KEPT IN RIGHT RELATION.

NOT NEUTRAL BY DEFAULT. NOT EVIL BY NATURE. BUT ALWAYS IN NEED OF JUST DIRECTION.

PROV. 27:12
THE PRUDENT SEES DANGER AND HIDES, BUT THE SIMPLE GO ON AND SUFFER FOR IT.

USE THE MACHINE. DO NOT WORSHIP IT.

Side margins hold cartouches (Idol/Tool/Hammer/Oracle on the left, Consequence with scales / Covenant on the right). A lower band has three numbered governance panels (Human with a kneeling figure, Institutional with a temple, Model with a ribcage-like skeletal motif), and a base row with a glowing red heart and a compass star. Palette is black, antique gold, teal, and crimson with ornate manuscript framing and heraldic corner roundels.
