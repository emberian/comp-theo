A dark, ominous poster styled as an infernal cathedral. The center features a glowing eye atop a towering altar/staircase of stacked feed-like panels, flanked by lit candelabra, evoking worship of an endless scroll. Below sits a radial diagram of "rites" (Recommended Loops, Comparison & Envy, Infinite Scroll, etc.) around a central red glow. Lower bands show a left-to-right machine pipeline and a four-cell "How the Feed Trains the Soul" row with small glowing icons. Palette is near-black with ember-gold and blood-red accents, ornate manuscript framing, and heraldic corner roundels.
