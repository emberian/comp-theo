## Transcription
XXV 25

QVOD VERUM EST, IN CORPORE VENIT.

ORDINARY MATTER AS GRACE

· COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE III ·

HELP OFTEN ARRIVES AS EMBODIED,
ORDINARY CONDITIONS RATHER THAN ABSTRACTION.

[Central ring nodes:]
FOOD
SLEEP
MEDICINE
SHELTER
TOUCH
CLEAN SPACE
RHYTHM
COMPANIONSHIP

GRACE DOES NOT REFUSE THE MATERIAL.
IT SANCTIFIES IT.

SYSTEMS CAN COORDINATE CARE, BUT CANNOT REPLACE PRESENCE.

PERSON
Show up.
Listen deeply.
Offer time.
Share resources.
Hold dignity.

COMMUNITY
Know your people.
Build trust.
Share burdens.
Create belonging.
Respond together.

MACHINE
Map needs.
Coordinate logistics.
Match resources.
Reduce delay.
Scale compassion.

IN PRACTICE

SEE
Notice real conditions.

DECIDE
Choose the kindest next thing.

ACT
Do something material.

REPEAT
Make it sustainable.

RECEIVE
Accept care without shame.

SOMETIMES SALVATION LOOKS LIKE
BREAD, LIGHT, REST, AND COMPANY.

· ✠ ·

[Left margin, top to bottom:]
DO NOT SPIRITUALIZE WHAT MUST FIRST BE FED.

FOOD
Nourishment sustains body and mind.

SLEEP
Rest restores rhythm and resilience.

MEDICINE
Healing treats what hurts.

SHELTER
Safety protects and grounds.

TOUCH
Presence conveys what words cannot.

CLEAN SPACE
Order and cleanliness quiet the mind.

COMPANIONSHIP
Shared presence makes the burden lighter.

RHYTHM
Regular practices anchor the days we keep.

[Right margin, top to bottom:]
GRACE OFTEN ARRIVES AS MATTER MADE KIND.

Light through the window.
A path to walk.
A song that steadies.
Clean water to drink.
A hand on the shoulder.
A door held open.

A CHATBOT MAY COMFORT, BUT CANNOT BECOME A HAND.

GOOD SYSTEMS AMPLIFY GOOD PEOPLE. THEY DO NOT REPLACE THEM.

## Visual
A dark engraved-style poster, gold and oxblood-red type on near-black, with an "XXV / 25" medallion top-left and a Latin motto banner across the top. The center is a large circular mandala: eight roundel icons (food, sleep, medicine, shelter, touch, clean space, rhythm, companionship) ringed around a warmly lit painted still life of bread and water on a table beneath an arched window. Below sit three icon-bulleted columns (PERSON / COMMUNITY / MACHINE) and a five-step "IN PRACTICE" row (SEE, DECIDE, ACT, REPEAT, RECEIVE), closing with a large two-line red-and-gold aphorism. Side margins carry framed roundel icons with short captions and a lamb-with-banner seal (lower left) and a red flame seal (lower right).
