## Visual
A dark, ominous devotional poster centered on a glowing many-armed deity figure (in the manner of a multi-armed idol) with a Σ symbol on its chest, radiating golden light. Its many hands hold bowls offering up labeled goods (Rest, Friendship, Truth, Body, Sleep, Play, Nuance, Tenderness, Minority Cases, Wonder) as sacrifices. Below are three numbered panels: a kneeling burnt-out figure (Human Sacrifices), a glowing classical building (Institutional Externalities), and a molecular graph beside scales of justice (Model Tradeoffs). The palette is black, fiery gold, and crimson with ornate manuscript framing and heraldic corner roundels.
