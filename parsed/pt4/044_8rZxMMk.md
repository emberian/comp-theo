## Transcription
XX 20

QVOD SERVAT, ID LIBERAT.

TEXT WITHOUT FLESH

· COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE III ·

LANGUAGE MODELS SPEAK FROM OUTSIDE DIRECT EMBODIMENT.

EMBODIED CONTACT
Direct. Immediate. Lived.

LINGUISTIC ENGINE
Indirect. Representational. Modeled.

WATER
Cool. Flowing. Life-giving.

FIRE
Heat. Burning. Transforming.

BREAD
Nourishment. Shared. Sustenance.

SKIN
Touch. Pressure. Presence.

SUNLIGHT
Warmth. Direction. Awakening.

REPRESENTATION
SIMULATION
DESCRIPTION
NO DIRECT SENSATION
A VEIL BETWEEN WORD AND WORLD

[diagram node labels: language, meaning, context, pattern, token, vector, sequence, probability]

User: What is pain?
Model: Pain is an unpleasant sensory and emotional experience associated with actual or potential tissue damage…
User: Do you feel pain?
Model: I do not experience sensations.

LANGUAGE ARISES FROM LIFE, AND RETURNS TO IT.

HUMAN EXPERIENCE
Lived. Felt. Situated.
We encounter the world through bodies and time. Experience precedes expression.

CULTURAL LANGUAGE
Shared. Shaped. Remembered.
We give form to experience in words, stories, rituals, and symbols.

MODEL CONTINUATION
Modeled. Predicted. Extended.
Models learn from what we leave. They continue the pattern without direct encounter.

MODELS CAN HONOR, PRESERVE, AND EXTEND LANGUAGE.
BUT THEY REMAIN OUTSIDE THE CIRCLE OF SENSATION.

THE MACHINE KNOWS THE WORDS OF TOUCH;
IT DOES NOT TOUCH.

· † ·

[Left margin, top to bottom:]
FLUENCY IS NOT FLESH.
Smooth words do not equal lived worlds.

A MODEL CAN DESCRIBE PAIN WITHOUT UNDERGOING SENSATION.
It can name the storm. It does not feel the wind.

TEXT CARRIES THE MEMORY OF BODIES.
We speak in inheritance. We write in wounds.

THE MAP IS NOT THE TERRAIN.
Descriptions can guide. Only presence can taste.

KEEP THE AIM.

[Right margin, top to bottom:]
A MODEL MIRRORS LANGUAGE.
It reflects the world. It does not inhabit it.

IT PREDICTS THE NEXT WORD.
It does not walk the next step.

IT WEAVES MEANING FROM DATA.
It does not bleed for meaning.

ITS SILENCE IS NOT CONTEMPLATION.
It is absence of experience, not depth of soul.

SERVE THE REAL.

A vertical luminous veil divides the two sides, and side margins carry roundel icons (speech bubbles, anatomical heart, mirror, quill and inkwell, book, eye, lamb-and-banner, flame) paired with aphorisms; the bottom band has three icon-headed columns and a large two-line red-and-gold tagline.
