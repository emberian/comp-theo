## Visual
A dark, intricately detailed engraving-style poster rendered in gold and black with a fine ornamental border and corner roundels containing small symbolic icons (eye, key, hourglass, scales). The central image is a towering, vertiginous gothic-cathedral city stacked into a single monumental ledger-temple, with a glowing rose-window sun behind a central spired tower and a small enthroned figure in a niche. Cascading staircases, arches, balconies, and vaulted galleries descend toward a bright doorway at the base, evoking an endless bureaucratic/ecclesiastical structure. Numerous small labeled cartouches float across the architecture, and a footer banner carries the series title beneath a triad of words.
