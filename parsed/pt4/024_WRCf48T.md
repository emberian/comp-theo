## Visual
An ornate gold-on-dark illustration in the style of an illuminated devotional poster. A luminous radiant dove descends at the top center over a vast classical city of domes, temples, and terraced gardens flanked by columned pavilions; below, a gathering of robed figures sits around a central altar table. Side columns hold paired captioned virtue panels with small icons, and a horizontal row of seven medallion symbols runs above an "Invocation" cartouche flanked by wax-seal emblems. The palette is amber, gold, and deep umber with dense baroque detailing and an arched, symmetrical sacred-architecture layout.
