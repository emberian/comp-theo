## Transcription
XXVII 27

VERITAS VIVIT IN CARNE. VERBUM FLUENT IN VERBO.

INCARNATE WISDOM
DISEMBODIED FLUENCY

· COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE III ·

BOTH SEEK THE GOOD. BOTH REVEAL PATTERNS.

ONE LIVES IT. ONE EXPRESSES IT.

AI CAN ASSIST SYMBOLIC REASONING.
IT DOES NOT BECOME ACCOUNTABLE IN THE SAME EMBODIED WAY.

HUMAN WISDOM
Embodied, mortal, accountable.
• Lives in a body that hungers, tires, and heals.
• Forms through practice, habit, and community.
• Weighs costs in the real.
• Answerable to persons and consequences.
• Learns by doing, refining, failing, repenting.
WISDOM IS RELATIONAL, MORTAL, AND PRACTICED.

CULTURAL MEMORY
Shared, transmitted, tested.
• Carries stories, laws, and songs.
• Preserves what matters across generations.
• Challenges and corrects error.
• Forms identity and shared goods.
• Adapts through discernment and tradition.
MEMORY IS THE BRIDGE BETWEEN LIFE AND TIME.

MACHINE FLUENCY
Disembodied, scalable, partial.
• Scales across data and domains without fatigue.
• Generates fluent continuations at speed.
• Optimizes for coherence and alignment.
• Not accountable for outcomes in the world.
• Improves by training, feedback, and iteration.
THE MODEL IS BRILLIANT AND PARTIAL.

TOGETHER, THEN:

USE WITH HUMILITY
Let tools serve wisdom, not the other way around.

VERIFY IN LIFE
Test in the world. Correct in community.

PROTECT THE VULNERABLE
Do not automate away empathy or responsibility.

SEEK THE GOOD
Pursue truth, beauty, and justice together.

REMAIN ACCOUNTABLE
We speak. We answer. We amend.

WHAT CAN SPEAK OF LIFE
IS NOT ALWAYS WHAT CAN LIVE IT.

· ✠ ·

[Left margin, top to bottom:]
THE EMBODIED PATH
Wisdom enters life through ordinary, faithful acts.

BREATHE — Receive. Be present.
EAT — Receive. Give thanks.
GRIEVE — Feel loss. Love remains.
SPEAK TRUTH — Name what is real.
CARE FOR OTHERS — Serve the vulnerable.

TRUTH MUST BE LIVED.
Wisdom is relational, mortal, and practiced.

EMBODIED DISCERNMENT
We are shaped by limits.
Time is finite.
Bodies are fragile.
Choices have cost.
Love takes effort.
Justice risks ourselves.
These limits make virtue possible.

LIFE IS THE SCHOOL OF WISDOM.

[Right margin, top to bottom:]
THE FLUENT PATH
Models pattern across vast symbolic space.

INGEST — Data in. Context in.
MODEL — Patterns form. Probabilities rise.
GENERATE — Words out. Paths branch.
ALIGN — With prompts. With aims.
SUGGEST — Continuations. Alternatives.

FLUENCY CAN ASSIST BUT NOT REPLACE LIFE.
The model is brilliant and partial.

MACHINE CAPACITY
We are shaped by scale.
Context is vast.
Recall is deep.
Permutation is limitless.
Clarity can be amplified.
Access can be widened.
These powers make service possible.

SERVICE IS THE AIM OF BOTH PATHS.
