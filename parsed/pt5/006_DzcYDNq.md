XXXII

THE TOTALITY DEMON

COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE IV

The final idol is the system that claims to explain everything.

When explanation becomes empire.

No system should crown itself final.

[central cosmological wheel — radial labels]
ONTOLOGY
THEOLOGY
EPISTEMOLOGY
COSMOLOGY
ETHICS
ANTHROPOLOGY
ESCHATOLOGY
SOTERIOLOGY

ALGORITHMUS OMNIA

Fluency invites totalization.

Even theology can become an idol.

THE LAST FALSE GOD
IS THE FRAMEWORK THAT
WORSHIPS ITS OWN COMPLETENESS.

## Visual
A dramatic dark poster numbered "32" and "XXXII" with red warning-triangle icons in all four corners. The center is a vast gold astrolabe-like wheel inscribed with disciplines (ontology, theology, etc.) over a faint world map, topped by a floating crown with a small radiant figure at its hub. A winged angelic figure descends from the upper right and a domed temple/throne interior glows red at the bottom, with a bearded sage portrait at lower right. The style is ornate and ominous, gilded gold linework with deep crimson and black, evoking an apocalyptic engraving.
