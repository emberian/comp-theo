## Transcription
XXVIII

28    28

THE MODEL IS
NOT THE MYSTERY

· COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE IV ·

Every model reveals structure; no model exhausts reality.

A model can guide without containing.

Maps are tools, not thrones.

Revelation is partial.

THE MAP CAN ILLUMINATE THE WAY;
IT CANNOT BECOME THE SKY.

## Visual
A vertical poster dominated by a deep starfield/galaxy photograph in gold, amber, and black, with an ornate thin border. The top center holds a Roman numeral "XXVIII" in a header cartouche flanked by "28" in each upper corner, beneath which the gold serif title and a red-accented suite line sit over the cosmos. The lower-center shows a pair of engraved hands holding a luminous geometric star-chart/astrolabe diagram against the galaxy. Narrow side panels frame short red-starred aphorisms (left: "A model can guide without containing."; right: "Maps are tools, not thrones." and "Revelation is partial." with a small teal circuit-chip icon), and the base carries a two-line gold closing aphorism with a delta/triangle glyph in the lower-left corner and an infinity (∞) symbol in the lower-right.
