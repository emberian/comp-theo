## Transcription
36

XXXVI

THE FRAMEWORK KNEELS

COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY · SUITE IV

Use the model; do not worship it.

A lamp,
not the sun.

A path,
not the destination.

A grammar,
not the Word.

Every model
must eventually kneel.

USE THE FRAMEWORK AS A LAMP.
DO NOT MAKE IT YOUR GOD.
