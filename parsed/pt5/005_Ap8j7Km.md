## Visual
A dark, gilded poster in an illuminated-manuscript style numbered "31" and "XXXI", with an arched architectural frame of columns and gold filigree. The central image shows a candle flame on a pedestal beside an upraised open hand radiating light, a gesture of refusal/blessing. Side panels carry a small molecular emblem and a shield icon; a lower row of four bordered panels uses engraved icons (a padlock, a question mark, a hooded figure, a sprouting plant) above their captions. The palette is black with antique gold, teal, and dark crimson accents and ornate serif typography.
