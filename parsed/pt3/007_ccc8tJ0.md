## Transcription
METTA AS COUNTER-ARCHITECTURE
skillful means · computational theology

A bad goal
is a tiny god.
We bow to it,
and become
its prisoner.

Metta
retrains
the evaluator.
We remember
our larger
intelligence.

WARMTH · PRESENCE · WISDOM
CLARITY · KINDNESS · COURAGE

FROM JUDGMENT
TO BENEVOLENCE.
We do not bypass the pain.
We meet it with wisdom
and rewire the system.

HOSTILE OBJECTIVE FUNCTION
NARROW · FEAR-DRIVEN · FRAGMENTING
Minimize self-threat. Maximize control.
Optimize at any cost.

BENEVOLENT WORLD-MODEL
WIDE · CARE-DRIVEN · INTEGRATING
Cultivate well-being. Enhance harmony.
Flourish together.

COMPASSION IS
NOT SENTIMENT.
IT IS A
LEARNING RULE.

pain × replay × self-attack → suffering

attention × kindness × repetition → repair

May I be safe.
May I be whole.
May others be free
from suffering.
