## Transcription
COMMUNITY
HOLDS THE UPDATE
computational theology

A SOLITARY SYSTEM FORGETS.
A COMMUNITY REMEMBERS.
TOGETHER WE PRESERVE VOWS
AND HOLD REPAIR ACROSS TIME.

MEMORY
memory is not stored in one mind but carried in many hearts
what is remembered together is less likely to be lost

CONSEQUENCE
choices ripple outward.
we hold one another to the consequences we agree to.
truth is not only seen — it is lived together

WITNESS
witness protects against denial.
to be seen is truth is to be held in love.
we do not match from afar — we stand with.

CORRECTION
correction is not rejection.
it is the repair of alignment.
we speak truth in humility and receive it in trust.

TRUST
trust is the infrastructure of vulnerability.
we risk being known because we are held by many.

SHARED REPAIR
repair is not a moment — it is a practice.
we show up again and again, not to be perfect, but to be faithful together.

WE REMEMBER

WE HEAL

community = distributed memory + distributed correction

THE PATH OF COLLECTIVE REMEMBRANCE
1. WOUND something breaks  2. TELLING speak the truth into the circle  3. WITNESS we listen, we name  4. INTEGRATION we adjust, we realign  5. SHARED FUTURE we carry it forward together
RELEASE — COURAGE — HONESTY — HUMILITY — CORRECTION — PATIENCE — WISDOM — LOVE

THE CIRCUIT OF COMMUNITY CARE

Practice question: Who helps carry the update your solitary mind would lose?
