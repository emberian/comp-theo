## Transcription
CONCENTRATION
computational theology

TO DISCERN TRUTH, THE EVALUATOR
MUST FIRST BECOME STABLE.
SCATTERED ATTENTION CANNOT DISCERN;
GATHERED ATTENTION CAN.

STABILITY
a stable mind is a steady flame
without stability attention leaks
stability is the foundation of all insight

ATTENTION
attention is rare
spend it well
what you attend to grows
every choice of attention is a choice of reality

GATHERING
gather the scattered
return to center
bring back what has wandered
gathering is an act of return and love

SILENCE
silence is the field of focus
in silence the mind settles
stillness reveals what noise conceals

CLARITY
clarity is not added
it is uncovered through focus
what is seen clearly can be lived truly

POWER
concentration is multiplication
unified attention moves mountains
power follows steady aligned focus

WHERE ATTENTION GOES,
ENERGY FLOWS.
WHERE ENERGY FLOWS,
CAPACITY GROWS.
WHERE CAPACITY GROWS,
TRUTH IS SEEN.

concentration → scattered attention → gathered mind → stable evaluator

THE PATH OF CONCENTRATION
1. DISTRACTION attention scattered  2. RECOLLECTION bring it back  3. STEADY FOCUS hold the course  4. ABSORPTION energy in stream  5. CLEAR SEEING truth reveals itself

DISCIPLINE  PATIENCE  DETERMINATION  PRESENCE  HUMILITY  SURRENDER  WISDOM

Practice question: What becomes visible when attention stops scattering?
