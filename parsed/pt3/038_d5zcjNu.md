## Transcription
PRAYER
computational theology

PRAYER IS NOT JUST REQUESTING OUTCOMES.
IT IS SHIFTING THE EVALUATOR
TOWARD A HIGHER CRITERION.

ADDRESS
speak to the One who sees fully
name what is real without hiding
bring the whole heart into words
honesty is the gateway

PRESENCE
prayer is first presence, not performance
be still and be known
the One who hears is already with you

SURRENDER
release the grip of control
offer the outcome into wiser hands
let go to be held by more than you

TRUST
trust is the consent to not knowing
you are wider in the hands of Love than in the prison of certainty

THE EVALUATOR TRANSFORMED
MAKE YOUR HEART
WIDER THAN THE EVENT

FROM MY VIEW TO THY VIEW
FROM MY WILL TO THY WILL
FROM MY JUDGMENT TO THY TRUTH

prayer = attention → address → revaluation

REVALUATION
ask not only for change, but for new eyes
let the event be seen from truth not from fear
higher criteria transforms the weights

HEIGHT
rise in view to see the whole
from a higher place, small things shrink, true things shine
from that height, respond in peace

THE PRACTICE OF PRAYER: SHIFTING THE EVALUATOR
1. TROUBLED HEART  2. ADDRESS IN PRAYER  3. LIGHT DESCENDS  4. STORY RE-SEEN  5. RESPONSE TRANSFORMED
a heavy confusion overwhelms — I speak you hear to here — grace enters truth clarifies — new context new meaning new vision — wisdom guides peace steadies inner steadiness

PRAYER PROCESS: SHIFTING THE CRITERION (LIGHTING CHART)
TROUBLED — ATTENTION — ADDRESS — DESCENT — RE-SEE — RESPOND
inner storm — turn the gaze inward — speak truth openly — grace received — craft new understanding — steadiness, responsible flow

HUMILITY  HONESTY  FAITH  TRUST  PATIENCE  SURRENDER  COURAGE  PEACE

Practice question: From what height does this event look different?
