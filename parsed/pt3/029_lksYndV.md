## Transcription
MERCY
computational theology

TRUTH IS SEEN • MERCY IS FREE
JUSTICE IS RIGHT • MERCY IS FINAL

TRUTH
names what is without distortion
clarity without cruelty
light that does not erase love

RELEASE
sets the prisoner free
removes what no longer serves
grace breaks every chain

TRUTH WEIGHS

MERCY DECIDES

SEAL

MENDED

REPAIR
mends what is broken
heals what was wounded
builds what can be redeemed

JUDGMENT
discerns with righteousness
weighs truth in context
mercy carries the final word

NONLOCAL CORRECTION
mercy reaches beyond the site of fault
sees the whole story
corrects without entrapment

RETURN
welcomes without shame
restores place and purpose
love completes what law begins

BURDEN LIFTED
DIGNITY RESTORED

ERROR IS REAL, BUT MERCY IS FINAL.
NO CONDEMNATION REMAINS WHERE MERCY REIGNS.

mercy = error → truth → release → repair

THE PATH OF MERCY: FROM ERROR TO RESTORATION

LOCAL ERROR

SEEN BY TRUTH

TRANSFORMED

RESTORATION

NOT CONDEMNED

reality fractures

truth illuminates

error repented and reoriented

life mended and renewed

no record of condemnation

Mercy does not ignore error. It enters it, transforms it, and ends it.

Justice establishes the boundary. Mercy crosses it by name.

As far as the east is from the west, so far does mercy remove what is repented. Ps 103:12

SEE MERCY   SPEAK TRUTH   HOLD LIGHT   CHOOSE MERCY   REDEEM LIFE   RENEW STORY   RESTORE ALL

Practice question: How can truth and tenderness coexist here?

## Visual
A gilded grimoire poster in devotional tone. The center shows a winged angel with arms outstretched between two balance scales ("Truth Weighs" / "Mercy Decides"), above two kneeling supplicant figures flanking a cracked, mending seal/heart motif. Symmetrical side panels (Truth, Repair, Nonlocal Correction on the left; Release, Judgment, Return on the right) frame the scene. A five-stage horizontal "Path of Mercy" diagram with glyphs runs across the lower portion, in warm gold, amber, and black illuminated-manuscript etching style; the scales and mending seal carry the truth-versus-mercy theme.
