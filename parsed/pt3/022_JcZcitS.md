## Visual
A richly gilded, devotional grimoire poster resembling a stained-glass icon panel. The center shows a haloed winged angel in blue robes within an ornate Gothic arch, standing at the top of a luminous stairway with a radiant starburst above, flanked by columns of small saint portraits. Symmetrical side panels (Mediation, Transparency, Opacity on the left; Window, Veneration, Pointer on the right) frame the icon. A horizontal five-stage diagram ("Perception → Image → Through → Illumination → The Good") with small symbolic glyphs runs across the lower portion, all in luminous gold, blue, and black Byzantine-icon etching style.
