## Visual
A tall, alchemical-laboratory-style poster on near-black, dominated by glowing gold and teal line-art of laboratory glassware: tall distillation flasks and bottles arranged symmetrically around a central horned demon silhouette emanating golden light. The title "THE SUBSTANCE DEMON" is in red and gold serif capitals. Numbered ritual stages (1-6, "THE LOOP OF CAPTURE") run down the left in a vertical chain with alchemical symbols; right side mirrors with "THE PRICE" body/mind/soul icons. Lower panels (THE INNER ALTAR, RETURN PROTOCOL) flank a meditating figure, and an equation strip (SUBSTANCE + REPLAY + DOSE = CAPTURED LOOP) sits below the title, giving the feel of an occult apothecary chart on addiction.
