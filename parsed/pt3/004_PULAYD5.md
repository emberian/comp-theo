## Visual
A tall, mystical poster on dark indigo-black depicting a luminous ascending human figure rising above a misty stone bridge over a chasm, with a second prone glowing figure lying at the bottom. The title "DISSOCIATION AS FALSE EXODUS" is in gold and red serif capitals. The composition is heavily symmetric with bracketed text panels down both margins and small line icons (eyes, bodies, sigils). A central horizontal "ROOT ADAPTATION" band of grey emblem cards and a lower "RETURN TO INCARNATION" row of glyph cards structure it as a flow, with cloud and ethereal smoke imagery reinforcing themes of detachment and return.
