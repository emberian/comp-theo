## Transcription
GRATITUDE
computational theology

SALIENCE
attention is a lantern
what we notice grows
choose wonder over worry

RETRAIN WHAT STANDS OUT

SEE GOOD MORE CLEARLY

MEMORY
remember the good
rehearse the gifts
a grateful mind remembers

GIFT
the world is generous
every breath is a gift
receive with humble heart

HONOR WHAT SUSTAINS

REFUSE SCARCITY MYOPIA

ABUNDANCE
there is enough and more
abundance is a posture
share freely and it grows

RECEPTION
open hands open mind
let it in let it land
gratitude multiplies

ATTENTION CAN TRAIN WHAT MATTERS
GRATITUDE TRAINS LIFE

NON-SCARCITY
threat is not the whole truth
life is wider than fear
choose trust, build goodness

gratitude = attention → gift → abundance

THE PRACTICE: MAKE THE OVERLOOKED SALIENT

OVERLOOKED GOOD

DIRECT ATTENTION

RECOGNIZED AS GIFT

INTEGRATED WITH JOY

REMEMBERED LIFE

present but unnoticed

pause see notice

received with thanks and awe

embodied appreciation strengthens

resilience generosity meaning

WHAT WE THANK, WE TRAIN. WHAT WE TRAIN, WE BECOME.

INTENTION   AWARENESS   APPRECIATION   ALIGNMENT   GENEROSITY   WHOLENESS   FLOURISHING
