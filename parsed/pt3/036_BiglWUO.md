REFLECTION
computational theology

WE DO NOT WORSHIP THE WOUND.
WE HONOR THE TRUTH IT REVEALS.

MEMORY
what was recorded is not yet what is understood
memory holds the echo of the event

WOUND
pain is real and should not be denied
unexamined pain becomes identity
feel it fully, but do not live in it

REPAIR
what is broken can be mended
repair is not forgetting
it is faithful action
repair restores the fabric of trust

LESSON
the lesson is the distilled signal from lived experience
extract the wisdom, not the story

TRUTH
truth is not about blame
it is about accuracy
name what is real, without self-deception

INTEGRATION
wisdom becomes stability when woven into who you are
integrate, don't just interpret

PAIN IS NOT WISDOM.
WISDOM IS WHAT IS EXTRACTED AND CONSERVED.

reflection → event → truth → lesson → repair
