RESENTMENT AS
DEBT ENFORCEMENT

THE UNPAID RECORD
This is a record kept — not only of acts, but of what was felt and never made right.

WHAT ANGER PROTECTS
Anger guards the value that was violated. It insists the wound meant something.

WHY THE MIND RETURNS
The mind returns to keep the debt visible. Repetition is remembrance without remedy.

NOT ALL WRONGS ARE REMEMBERED.

YET ALL UNRELEASED WRONGS ARE CARRIED.

BINDING THE FUTURE TO THE WOUND
Carried debt distorts itself forward, shaping expectation, relationship, and self-worth.

TRUTH WITHOUT RELEASE
The pursuit may be true, but truth not surrendered becomes a chain even willingly.

NOT ALL DEBTS CAN BE ERASED. BUT ALL DEBTS CAN BE HANDED TO THE RIGHT COURT.

WRIT OF JUST REMEMBRANCE
Every wrong was endured. Personal records. Dignity. Debts. Love witness. Christ defenders. Repentance. Remaining.

THE DEBTOR
THE ENFORCER Bound to remember. Sworn keeper. Vigilant before.

BOTH ARE BOUND BY WHAT IS REFUSED RELEASE

ACCUSATION — ACCOUNTABILITY

Resentment often arises as the soul's attempt
to keep a real debt from disappearing,
even when that vigilance becomes another bondage.

TO RELEASE IS NOT TO DENY. IT IS TO YIELD THE WEIGHT TO TRUTH AND TRUST.

BRING THE UNRELEASED TO THE COURT OF LIGHT.
NAME IT | SURRENDER IT | BE FREE WITHIN IT

WHAT IS CHAINED TO THE WOUND CANNOT SERVE THE CALLING.
