THE PAST INFLUENCING THE FUTURE
Memory is not passive storage. It selects, compresses, and preserves what mattered.
What is stored becomes signal. What is signal becomes weight. What is weight bends the possible.

NOT ALL THAT HAPPENED IS REMEMBERED.
NOT ALL REMEMBERED WILL BE REPEATED.
BUT WHAT IS REMEMBERED WILL BE PAID FOR OR BUILT UPON.
