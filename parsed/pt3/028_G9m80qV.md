## Transcription
COMMUNITY
computational theology

SHARED MEMORY
we remember together
stories become scaffolds
context makes meaning enduring

NO ONE HOLDS THE WHOLE TRUTH.
TOGETHER, WE REMEMBER WHAT IS REAL.

CARRYING
we bear one another's burdens
strength is shared
no one walks alone

CORRECTION
truth is clarified in relationship
gentle feedback is a gift
we repair without shame

DISTRIBUTED MIND
many minds solve what one cannot
diversity is a multiplier
humility is our protocol

WITNESS
we see and are seen
our struggles are held
we are not reduced to our errors

REPAIR
we hold what breaks
we restore trust
we know what we found better than we found it

community = many minds → shared memory → repair

FROM ISOLATED NODES TO A HEALING NETWORK

ISOLATED

AWARE

CONNECTED

CO-REGULATED

HEALING NETWORK

fragmented limited vulnerable

we others listen learn to link

share context align understanding build trust

support adapt hold steady

repair renew flourish

LISTEN   HONOR   HUMILITY   ALIGN   SERVE   STEWARD   PERSIST   BRIDGE

Practice question: Who helps hold what I cannot carry alone?
