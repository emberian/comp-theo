VIPASSANA
computational theology

WHAT LOOKED LIKE FATE,
BECOMES A DISCERNIBLE CHAIN.

OBSERVE
seeing is the beginning of freedom
notice without grasping
awareness is the lamp

DISTINGUISH
things are not the same
name clearly what is present
discernment breaks illusion

LINK
each link conditions the next
nothing stands alone
understanding sees the chain

CRAVING
clinging builds the loop
wanting becomes craving
craving binds the mind

FEAR
fear tightens the loop
avoidance multiplies suffering
to feel, see, allow, release

ACTION
actions arise from causes
intention shapes becoming
mindful action creates freedom

RELEASE
let go of identification
freedom is not escape
release is wisdom
