REPLAY: LEARNING REOPENS THE WOUND
The event ends; the training signal returns.

1. WHAT IS EXPERIENCE REPLAY
Experience replay is the process of reactivating important past episodes—real or imagined—so the system can learn from them again.
The past is sampled so the future can adapt.

2. WHY REPLAY IS USEFUL
- IMPROVES VALUE ESTIMATION | Corrects biased or noisy predictions.
- IMPROVES PLANNING | Supplies diverse past trajectories for better decisions.
- REFINES MODELS | Updates internal models of dynamics, transitions, and consequences.
- BUILDS ROBUSTNESS | Strengthens performance under uncertainty and novelty.

3. WHY REPLAY HURTS
Replayed events are partially treated as live data. The body reacts. The system re-learns pain, fear, and failure—even when the event is no longer happening.
REPLAY COSTS
Reactivates affective intensity
Consumes cognitive resources
Biased sampling increases overweighting
Harder to update beliefs under high arousal

4. THE REPLAY CYCLE
EVENT (Something happens.) -> MEMORY TRACE (Encoded with creation + context.) -> REPLAY (Sampled from memory.) -> UPDATED MODEL (Beliefs, values, and policies are updated.) -> STRONGER SALIENCE (More available, more likely to replay.)

5. TRAUMA LOOP
High-salience failure or threat occurs -> Replayed more often -> Becomes more available -> Stays likely to be replayed in the future
Repetition deepens the present.

6. COUNTERFACTUAL REPLAY
The mind simulates alternatives to update learning.
"What should have happened?" Correctly predicted errors. Builds better models.
"What could I have done?" Explores actions. Improves future agency.
"What may happen again?" Prepares for uncertainty. Builds scenario reserves.
Counterfactuals turn regret into adaptive insight—if held well.

7. LIBERATING REPLAY
Not all replay binds us. Some replay frees us.
COMPLIANCE REPLAY (RUMINATION) -> RESOLUTION REPLAY (INTEGRATION)
Involuntary -> Intentional
Emotion-driven -> Value-guided
Forever, stuck loop -> Bound, flexible view
Amplifies pain -> Extracts meaning
No update -> Updates beliefs
Deepens the wound -> Heals the wound
Reacts -> Learns
Repeats -> Changes
Choose the mode. Change the outcome.
