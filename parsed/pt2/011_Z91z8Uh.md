METACOGNITIVE LIBERATION
The system suffers less when it can observe its own suffering-process.

1. METACOGNITION
Metacognition is cognition about cognition.
The system can notice what it is thinking, feeling, expecting, desiring, and doing.
This creates a vantage point separate from the contents.

2. ATTENTION AS CURRICULUM
What the system repeatedly notices becomes what it learns.
- ATTENTION SELECTS | What enters the field.
- REPETITION STRENGTHENS | What is rehearsed.
- WHAT'S PRACTICED | Becomes automatic.
- CHANGE CURRICULUM | Change the system.

3. DRAWING FINER DISTINCTIONS
Suffering fuses many things into one wave. Metacognition separates them into events.
STIMULUS | THOUGHT | ATTACHMENT | AVERSION | REPLAY
These can now be seen, named, and investigated individually rather than reacted to as a single, fused process.

4. REDUCING THE TERMS
Suffering intensifies when certain terms are rigid. Each term can be softened.
EXPECTATION: rigid demand <-> flexible forecast
CERTAINTY: must be true <-> may be true
AVERSION: narrow fixation <-> open field
REPLAY: ruminative loop <-> brief revisit
Soft terms reduce amplification without losing function.

5. THE SECOND ARROW
The first arrow is the felt impact of reality: pain, loss, failure.
The second arrow is mental resistance: aversion, blame, self-judgment, replay.
Pain may be unavoidable. Extra suffering is optional.

6. MINDFULNESS
Mindfulness is observing thoughts and emotions as events, not commands.
NOTICE (What is happening?) -> LABEL (What is this?) -> FEEL (Allow the sensation.) -> RELEASE (Let it move on.)
This creates space between event and reaction. Space is freedom.

7. ACCEPTANCE
Acceptance is an open stance toward what is, without suppressing data.
- See clearly what is present.
- Allow it to be present.
- Learn from it.
- Act from values, not reflex.
Acceptance preserves learning while reducing torment.

8. NOT ABOLISHING ERROR
Metacognition does not delete learning signals. It changes the relationship to them.
- Errors are still detected.
- Signals still carry information.
- Updates still occur.
- The difference: less reactivity, more stability, clearer choice.

FROM REACTIVE LOOP TO OBSERVED LOOP
REACTIVE LOOP (Suffering amplifies): STIMULUS -> INTERPRET -> REACT -> REPLAY -> MORE SUFFERING. Automatic. Fast. Habitual.
OBSERVED LOOP (Learning deepens, suffering reduces): STIMULUS -> NOTICE -> INQUIRE -> RESPOND -> WISE LEARNING. Aware. Flexible. Liberating.

Liberation begins when the learner can study the machinery of its own pain.
