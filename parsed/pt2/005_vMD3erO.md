DESIRE = REACHABILITY + REWARD

A state becomes wanted when it appears both good and attainable.

Desire is the mind’s  
process of generating  
and prioritizing possible  
future states as goals.

It selects states that  
appear both valuable  
and within reach

NOT ALL AFFORDED STATES BECOME DESIRED.

Agents and institutions carve the landscape.
