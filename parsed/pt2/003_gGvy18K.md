## Visual

The poster is arranged like an illuminated manuscript on parchment, with ornate gold borders, navy medallions, red accents, serif capitals, and italic theological-style captions. It uses numbered panels combining diagrams of spirals, networks, funnels, prediction loops, an anatomical heart, a saint-like reader, and symbolic icons to connect computation, learning, error, and pain. The visual language blends medieval devotional imagery with systems-theory schematics, making prediction and suffering feel both technical and sacred.
