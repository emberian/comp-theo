PAINFUL AUTODIDAXIS
How a self-learning universe becomes intelligent, wounded, and reflective.

1. CORE THESIS
An autodidactic universe learns from experience. But once it predicts, desires, and evaluates outcomes, intelligence acquires the structural possibility of pain.

2. AUTODIDAXIS
Self-learning without an external teacher.
- COMPRESSION | Finds shorter descriptions that preserve predictive power.
- REGULARITY DETECTION | Discerns stable patterns across variation.
- SELF-SAMPLING | Generates experiences to test and refine its model.
- CONSEQUENCE MEMORY | Stores what happened, in what context, with what result.

3. PAINFUL INTELLIGENCE
Pain is the felt side of frustrated prediction and failed reward expectation.
Error signals necessary for learning can become suffering when they matter to a self.

INDEX EQUATION
pain = perceived discrepancy × salience × replay
WHERE:
perceived discrepancy = | expected − actual |
salience = degree of self-relevance or value impact
replay = frequency of reactivation / rehearsal

4. THE NEW SYNTHESIS
WORLD → EXPERIENCE → MODEL → EXPECTATION → ACTION → OUTCOME → ERROR SIGNAL → LEARNING
When error becomes salient, self-relevant, and replayed, learning becomes painful.

5. THREE PRIMITIVE OPERATORS
- DISTINCTION: Marks differences. Creates the first inside / outside, same / different, this / that.
- PREDICTION: Projects the future from the model. Opens vulnerability to being wrong.
- REPLAY: Reactivates past experience. Multiplies salience and deepens its imprint.

6. WHAT THIS SERIES EXPLORES
- Prediction: the engine of intelligence
- Desire: attraction to the possible good
- Frustration: when the good is blocked
- Replay: the amplifier of pain
- Self-models: who is the learner?
- Blowtorch-shaped desire: intensity that can heal or harm
- Metacognition: knowing that you know (and that you suffer)
- Contemplative intelligence: seeing clearly without adding harm

7. KEY BOUNDARY
LEARNING LAWS: Understanding what is true.
Objective. Causal. Value-neutral.
LEARNING HOW THOSE LAWS HURT: Understanding how truth meets preference and desire.
Subjective. Particular. Value-laden.

WORLD SPIRAL: Reality unfolds. W_t ∈ Ω
NETWORK OF LAW: Regularities connect. L = {l_1, l_2, ..., l_n}
MEMORY FUNNEL: Compress, retain, index. M_t = f(M_{t-1}, e_t)
COMPARISON BOX: ŷ → y
CAUSAL LOOP: Learning closes the loop. World → Model → World

A universe becomes intelligent when it predicts. It becomes painful when prediction matters.
