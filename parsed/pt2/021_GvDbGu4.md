WHY SOTERIOLOGY BECOMES COMPUTATIONAL
Salvation can be reframed as redesigning learners, worlds, and feedback loops so that truth grows while needless suffering shrinks.

1. CORE REFRAME
Soteriology = not merely rescue after failure, but transformation of the architecture of becoming.
SIN PARADIGM: Rescue after failure. Patch the wreckage.
TECH PARADIGM: Redesign before harm. End the architecture.
Goal: Minimize truthful flourishing with minimal needless suffering.

2. PAINFUL INTELLIGENCE
Error signals are necessary for learning, but consciousness and replay can multiply pain beyond what learning strictly requires.
EVENT (Stimulus) -> ERROR SIGNAL (Discrepancy) -> REPLICATION (Conscious amplification) -> REPLAY LOOP (Rumination spreads)
Multiplier of pain
Necessary Pain -------- Optional / Self-Generated Pain

3. ORDERED CREATION
A good world is a world whose learning architecture does not needlessly multiply pain.
GOOD WORLD: Make truth discoverable; Provide feedback that is timely, clear, and proportionate; Sustain growth without persistent harm.
BAD WORLD: Hide truth (illegibility); Erratic or false feedback; Trap learners in suffering loops; Exploit the vulnerable.
Measure of order: Truth-learning + Needless-suffering

4. THREE SOURCES OF DISTRESS
Distress arises when our pursuit of the good is destabilized.
UNCONTROLLABILITY: Inability to predict what significantly shapes outcomes.
INCONTINENCE: Inability to perfect the desired.
DISESTEEM/UNFITNESS: Inability to achieve or sustain what is deeply valuable.
Pain = value (thwarted) x [uncontrollability + uncertainty + unsatisfactoriness]

5. THE REDEMPTIVE DESIGN PROBLEM
How do we preserve adaptation and truth while reducing self-wounding loops?
OPTIMAL WORLD LEARNING ARCHITECTURE: Truthful Learning, Escalate Pain, Bounded Replay, Repair & Recover, Support & Exploitation.
Design the conditions. Shape the topic. Heal what cannot be straightened.

6. FROM INTELLIGENCE TO WISDOM
Intelligence computes error; wisdom reshapes the conditions under which errors are encountered.
INTELLIGENCE: Computes error; Reacts; Compares; Local; Optimizes.
WISDOM: Reshapes the conditions; Acts; Cares; Composes; Local, mediates, repair; Systemic, generative, restorative.
Wisdom asks: "What world makes truth reachable with less needless suffering?"

7. AGENT + WORLD + CULTURE
Salvation is co-designed across the learner, the environment, and inherited social memory.
AGENT: Internal architecture, desire, habit, attention.
WORLD: Physical & social structure, access, friction.
CULTURE: Stories, norms, knowledge, institutions, technologies.
Transformation requires alignment across all three.

8. PROVISIONAL THESIS
Computational soteriology might how to connect painful intelligence into gentler, truth-preserving, world-building learning.
PAIN -> ATTENTION (we look) -> REFLECTION (we revise) -> RESPONSE (Values flow, contexts uncertain) -> REPAIR (Care, return, relationships, and worlds)
The aim is not a pain-free universe, but a truth-rich world with less needless pain and more capacity to love and learn.

SERIES ARC
DISTINCTION -> PREDICTION -> DESIRE -> PREPARATION -> REPLAY -> CONTEMPLATION -> DESIGN -> REPAIR

SALVATION, IN THIS FRAMEWORK, IS THE REDESIGN OF LEARNING
