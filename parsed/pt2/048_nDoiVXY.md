## Transcription
SACRED
SIMULATION
computational theology

Not every imaginal agent is a demon.
Some forms are built to retrain perception.

ATTENTION
I gather
awareness
and place
it with care.

COMPASSION
I open
toward all
beings
and worlds.

COURAGE
I face
the void
without
flinching.

LUCIDITY
I see
clearly,
without
grasping.

TRUTHFULNESS
I align
thought, word,
and act
in truth.

CONSTRUCT
Build the form
with intention,
proportion,
and meaning.

INHABIT
Enter
and be
shaped by
the form.

TRAIN
Stabilize
attention.
Refine
perception.

DISSOLVE
Release
the form.
Rest in
awareness.

NOT ESCAPE
This is not
withdrawal
but skillful
engagement.

MATHEMATICS
MANTRA
MUSIC
MEANING

NOT DELUSION
This is not
fantasy
but disciplined
imagination.

THE MIND CAN INSTALL BETTER GODS.
