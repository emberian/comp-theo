EXORCISM
AS DE-IDOLIZATION
computational theology

The demon is not defeated by hatred.
It is starved by the withdrawal of worship.

REDUCE
ATTENTION
Withdraw the gaze.
Attention is
nourishment.
Look less.
Be free more.

REDUCE
CERTAINTY

the chains
of false
certainty.
Let mystery
breathe.

REDUCE
REPLAY

the story.
Break the loop.
The past is
not your prison.

REDUCE
SELF-BINDING
You are not
the story.
You are not
the role.
You are not
the idol.
You are free.

NOT TRUTH
