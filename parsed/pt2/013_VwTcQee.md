PAIN BECOMES CURRICULUM
A self-learning system reorganizes itself around what hurt, what mattered, and what returned.

1. CORE CLAIM
Pain is not mere damage; it is error made salient to a self.
pain = discrepancy x self-relevance x replay
(error) (relevance) (revisited)

2. WHY PAIN TEACHES
Surprising loss violates expectation and demands attention.
It forces policy adjustment to reduce future error.
It prioritizes memory so the system doesn't forget what mattered.

3. SALIENCE RANKING
WEAK EVENTS (Ignored): Low impact, no update.
STRONG EVENTS (Stored): Moderate impact, one-pass learning.
PAINFUL EVENTS (Repeatedly Sampled): High impact, multi-pass learning.
Salience = (error x self-relevance) + (1 + #replays)

4. CURRICULUM SHIFT
World (context) -> Event (happens) -> Loss (error) -> Attention (focus)
Replay (revisit) -> Policy Update (adjust) -> Future Avoidance (steer away) -> Future Curriculum (re-weighted)

5. PAINFUL INTELLIGENCE
When prediction, desire, and self-modeling are fused, intelligence feels pain.
PREDICTION (what will happen)
DESIRE (what I want to happen)
SELF-MODEL (who I am, what matters)
PAIN

6. ADAPTIVE BENEFIT / MALADAPTIVE RISK
PAIN AS SIGNAL: Highlights critical error; Guides better choices; Prioritizes what matters; Builds resilience through calibration.
PAIN AS PRISON: Amplifies noise; Feeds rumination; Systems attention; Erodes well-being and flexibility.
Wisdom = extract signal, abandon the prison.

7. WHAT GETS LEARNED?
- What to avoid (reduce future loss)
- What to seek (increase future gain)
- What threatens identity (self-model defense)
- What predicts future loss (anticipate & prepare)

8. PROVISIONAL THESIS
The system learns fastest where it is:
- Vivid (high surprise)
- Self-relevant (this matters to me)
- Repeatedly revisited (on purpose or by compulsion)
Learning models with signal x relevance x replay.

SYNTHESIS: THE PAIN CURRICULUM LOOP
SIGNAL: Error becomes salient.
MEMORY: Encoded and revisited.
POLICY: Beliefs and behaviors are updated.
FUTURE: Lens of the self past, more of what matters.

Pain becomes curriculum when the learner starts studying the wound more than the world.
