A lawful world need not be just. But it can still be learnable. Learnability makes repair possible.

Calling suffering a skill issue is not blaming the sufferer. It means the dynamics of pain are partly trainable and partly designable.

Theodicy becomes more practical when the question shifts from "Why pain?" to "Which local skills and structures keep frustration from becoming torment?"
