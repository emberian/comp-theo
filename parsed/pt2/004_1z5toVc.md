PREDICTION IS THE FALL

The moment a system expects, reality can disappoint it.

1  
INNOCENCE: NON-PREDICTIVE IMMEDIACY

Innocence is the state of  
non-predictive presence.  
Experience is received  
without projection,  
without expectation,  
without the shadow  
of an imagined future.

Innocence = perception without expectation.

2  
PREDICTION: MODEL-BASED ANTICIPATION

Prediction is the use of a  
model to anticipate what  
will (likely) happen next.  
It projects a future state  
into being—an expected  
future—before it occurs.

Future Tree

Prediction = model + projection → expected future.

3  
THE SPLIT: EXPECTED VS ACTUAL — THE WOUND

EXPECTED (E)  
The future the model  
said would happen.

ACTUAL (A)  
The future that  
actually happens.

GAP = WOUND

When reality arrives, it rarely  
matches the expectation.  
The discrepancy is the wound.

\Delta = actual - expected

| expected - actual |

6  
WHY INTELLIGENCE  
NEEDS IT

Prediction turns the infinite  
into the manageable.

COMPRESSION  
Summarize the future

ACTION SELECTION  
Choose the best path

EFFICIENT LEARNING  
Use feedback to update

Without prediction, intelligence

Once the future is imagined,  
blocked futures become  
emotionally and  
computationally real.

Suffering is the price of a future  
we can imagine but cannot guarantee.
