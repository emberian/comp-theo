## Transcription
COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY SERIES - 3 / 9

BLOWTORCH COSMOGENESIS
Directed energy, carved voids, and the active formation of cosmic structure.

1. THE CLAIM
Early supermassive black-hole jets actively shape the cosmic web rather than leaving structure formation to passive gravity alone.

2. VOIDS ARE FORMED ABSENCES
Jets excavate cavities in the primordial medium. What we see as vast emptiness may be the result of prior energetic clearing, not merely a lack of matter.

3. FILAMENTS AND BOUNDARIES
As cavities expand and meet, matter is swept, compressed, and left behind in filaments and shells. Boundaries become sharply defined where cleared regions collide.

4. JET → CAVITY → FIELD → FLOW
1. JET: Relativistic jets from an active nucleus punch into the surrounding medium. Directed energy is launched.
2. CAVITY: Jets inflate and carve cavities, evacuating and displacing gas. Space is actively cleared.
3. FIELD: Currents and turbulence imprint magnetic fields along the cavity walls. Fields are inscribed.
4. FLOW: Magnetized channels guide later plasma and matter, sculpting the cosmic web. Structure is guided and defined.
A single episode of directed energy can shape structure on vast scales.

5. MAGNETIC TRAM TRACKS
Magnetic field lines make some directions cheaper than others. Plasma follows paths of least resistance, producing anisotropic channels and sheets.
- Easy (along field)
- Hard (across field)
The universe is not isotropic in practice.

6. ACTIVE ASYMMETRY
PASSIVE AGGREGATION: Gravity pulls. Matter drifts. Structure slowly emerges.
VS.
DIRECTED FORMATION (ENERGY): Energy acts. Space is carved. Structure is imposed.
Blowtorch Cosmogenesis is a model of active, asymmetric world-making.

7. THEOLOGICAL READING
- Creation by carving: Being is made by energetic incision.
- Distinction by evacuation: Emptiness is a worked boundary, not mere non-being.
- World-formation by selective clearing: Order arrives through directed acts that open space for life.

8. MORAL WARNING
Formative violence can create order without being just.
- Power that carves cavities can also devastate them.
- End state not automatically justify nature.
- Discernment is required to distinguish creative from cruel.
- Justice must govern the use of power that shapes worlds.

BLOWTORCH FORMATION GIVES US A MODEL OF CREATION AS ACTIVE CHANNEL-CARVING, NOT MERE PASSIVE ACCRETION.
