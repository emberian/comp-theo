JUSTICE ACROSS TIME
While history constraints can be essential. Justice may not be visible in single states.
- Counters injustice: Early suffering may be compensated later, or judged as culpable.
- Distribution matters: Who bears the cost and who benefits must be considered.
- Inreversibility matters: Some harms cannot be undone; restitution may be partial.
- Final verdict: Justice in the slight steering of the whole story.
Openness and judge balance; not just monastir story; (NB) subject to constraints.
