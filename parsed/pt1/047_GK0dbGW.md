THE ACTION LANDSCAPE IS REWRITTEN
Blowtorch processes do not merely choose paths — they edit future path costs.

1. BASIC IDEA
Early energetic events reshape the future geometry of action. By carving structure, setting gradients, and opening or closing routes, they change what will be easy, hard, or impossible for everything that comes after.

2. FROM STRUCTURE TO COST
Physical structure becomes economic structure: cavities, field lines, and boundaries alter the local transition cost function for all future moves.
- CAVITIES: create detours or shortcuts
- FIELD LINES: channel or resist flow
- BOUNDARIES: permit, block, or tax crossing
These features become costs in the action functional.

4. LOW-ACTION CHANNELS
Structure creates conduits where the effective cost of movement is greatly reduced. Flow is funneled, accelerated, and made robust against disturbance. These channels become dynamically cheap and therefore heavily preferred by any rational system.
- Preferred flow
- Alternative paths
- Impeded path

5. RESERVOIRS AND DUMPS
Costs can be externalized into regions that absorb, hide, or sequester action. These reservoirs and dumps keep visible pathways cheap by paying the cost elsewhere.
- RESERVOIR: stores cost out of sight
- DUMP / VOID: discharged that absorbs loss

6. NEGATIVE CREATION
- CLEARING: remove obstacles
- EXCLUDING: keep what should not act
- BLOCKING: forbid destructive routes
- EVACUATING: pull cost into safe regions
- MAKING ROOM: create capacity for future life

7. TROPICAL INTUITION
Action accumulates by min-plus algebra. We add costs along a path; then minimax over all alternatives.
1. Path cost (tropical sum): C(π) = ⊕(t(x_t → x_{t+1})) = Σ t_t
2. Best path (tropical min): C*(x → y) = ⋀_π C(π) = min_π C(π)
3. Compose (tropical product): C(x→y) = ⊗_t = C(x→z) ⊗ C(z→y)
Result: the same algebra; min-plus structure.

8. WHY IT MATTERS
A world may look lawful, stable, and efficient not because the agents are micromanaged, but because the action landscape was preconditioned in advance.
- Paths look natural because they really are cheaper
- Order persists because disorder is expensive
- Freedom operates inside a designed cost field
- History unfolds inside the shadow of earlier edits.
