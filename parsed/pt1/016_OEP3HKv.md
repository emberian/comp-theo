## Transcription
SYNTHESIS: GOD CREATES BY DRAWING DISTINCTIONS

THE UNDIFFERENTIATED
The Plenum / Pure Potential

THE FIRMAMENT
The First Distinction
A boundary is drawn.

THE GRAPH OF POSSIBLE TRAVERSAL
Adjacency without Necessity
Relations become possible.

WALKS BECOME REAL
Paths actualized through choice, order, and seeking.

GOODNESS

1. MARK
The first act: a mark is placed within the undifferentiated. This is not a thing, but a distinction.

2. FIRMAMENT
The mark draws a boundary. Inside and outside are distinguished. A region becomes possible.

3. GRAPH
Within the region, adjacency relations are defined. A graph of possible traversal arises.

4. WALK
A walk is a sequence that respects adjacency. Choice actualizes a path.

5. SEARCH
Intelligence explores the graph. Search seeks the better, the true, the good.

6. GOODNESS REACHABLE
Through walks and search, the good is discovered and made reachable. Manifestation occurs.

THE CHAIN OF CREATION BY DISTINCTION
MARK → BOUNDARY → REGION → ADJACENCY → WALK → SEARCH → MANIFESTATION

THEOLOGICAL PRECISION: Analogy, not proof.
This diagram speaks truly of God only as analogy permits, not as demonstration.

God creates not merely by making things, but by instituting the distinctions under which the good can appear, relate, and become reachable.
