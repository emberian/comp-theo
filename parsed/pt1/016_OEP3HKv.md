## Transcription

SYNTHESIS:

GOD CREATES  
BY  
DRAWING DISTINCTIONS

1. MARK

The first act:  
a mark is placed  
within the undifferentiated.  
This is not a thing,  
but a distinction.

THE UNDIFFERENTIATED  
The Plenum / Pure Potential

THE FIRMAMENT  
The First Distinction  
A boundary is drawn.

4. WALK

A walk is a sequence  
that respects adjacency.

Choice actualizes  
a path.

2. FIRMAMENT

The mark draws a boundary.  
Inside and outside  
are distinguished.  
A region becomes possible.

THE GRAPH OF POSSIBLE TRAVERSAL  
Adjacency without Necessity.  
Relations become possible.

5. SEARCH

Intelligence explores  
the graph.  
Search seeks  
the better, the true,  
the good.

3. GRAPH

Within the region,  
adjacency relations  
are defined.  
A graph of possible  
traversal arises.

WALKS BECOME REAL  
Paths actualized through choice,  
order, and seeking.

GOODNESS

6. GOODNESS  
REACHABLE

Through walks and search,  
the good is discovered  
and made reachable.  
Manifestation occurs.

THE CHAIN OF CREATION BY DISTINCTION

MARK → BOUNDARY → REGION → ADJACENCY → WALK → SEARCH → MANIFESTATION

THEOLOGICAL PRECISION: Analogy, not proof.  
This diagram speaks truly of God only as analogy permits, not as demonstration.

God creates not merely by making things,  
but by instituting the distinctions under which  
the good can appear, relate, and become reachable.

## Visual

A dark navy and gold illuminated-manuscript style poster is organized around a central vertical cosmological diagram, flanked by six numbered explanatory panels. The center imagery descends from a galaxy-like undifferentiated plenum to a glowing boundary disk, graph network, winding walk, and radiant “GOODNESS” node. Gold line art, serif typography, thin ornamental borders, stars, graph nodes, arrows, a magnifying glass, and radiant bursts visually encode distinction, adjacency, search, and manifestation.
