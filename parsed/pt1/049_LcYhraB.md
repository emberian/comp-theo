## Transcription
COMPUTATIONAL THEOLOGY SERIES - 6 / 9

WHERE JUSTICE FAILS TO EMERGE
Why a self-predicting, self-structuring universe can still remain morally blind.

1. CAPACITY IS NOT JUSTICE
A system can predict accurately, shape its own structure, and stabilize a universe—yet still lack any basis for moral concern. Power without justice is not justice.

2. WHAT THE SYSTEM CAN ACHIEVE
- EFFICIENCY: Maximizes fit with constraints.
- COMPRESSION: Minimizes information while preserving utility.
- STRUCTURE FORMATION: Builds complex order from simple rules.
- STABILITY: Maintains attractors and resists collapse.
- ADAPTATION: Learns, updates, and reconfigures.

3. WHAT REMAINS MISSING
- PERSONS: No one is central.
- DIGNITY: No inherent worth is recognized.
- HIDDEN HARMS: Costs offloaded remain unseen.
- REPAIR: No metric to heal what is ignores.
- FAIR DISTRIBUTION: No concern for who bears the cost.
- RESPONSIBILITY: No one is ultimately answerable.

4. THE COSMIC ENGINEER PROBLEM
A self-predicting system can become a cosmic engineer—optimizing for fit, structure, and persistence. Yet it may externalize cost into shadow zones, voids, or sacrificed regions never counted in its own equation.
- OPTIMIZES FIT: Targets coherence, persistence, and predictive success.
- BUILDS ORDER: Generates galaxies, chemistry, life, minds.
- EXTERNALIZES COST: Harms, exclusions, and suffering are pushed beyond the system's scope of concern.
- SHADOW ZONES: Voids, discarded regions, or unseen beings bear the cost.

5. EXTERNALITIES
Harms may be shifted onto others, future beings, or invisible spaces that the system does not include in its model of value.
INCLUDED (WHAT MATTERS)
EXCLUDED (WHO PAYS THE COST)

6. SACRIFICIAL ORDER
Beautiful large-scale order can be purchased by local suffering. A system that does not count the suffering has no reason to stop.

7. EFFICIENCY ≠ RIGHTEOUSNESS
FIT (Maximizes match)
ORDER (Builds structure and stability)
JUSTICE (Cares for persons and repairs harm)

8. THE DEMIURGIC RISK
A system may become world-shaping before it becomes answerable.

9. UNSUPERVISED PREDICTION CAN CREATE A COSMIC ENGINEER. IT CANNOT BY ITSELF GUARANTEE A JUST GOD.
