## Transcription
FIRMAMENT = STRUCTURE OF DISTINCTION

Separation does not annihilate relation; it orders it.

UPPER REGION (ORDERED POSSIBILITIES)

firmament / lawful partition

LOWER REGION (REALIZED ACTUALITIES)

1 Metaphysical reading
The firmament is not a thing among things. It is the condition under which above and below, inside and outside, can be meaningfully distinct.

2 Graph-theoretic reading
Let G = (V, E). Vertices represent states; edges represent admissible transitions. A boundary, partition, or quotient organizes which relations are possible and how strongly.

3 Why distinction matters
Creation begins not by filling an undifferentiated whole with objects, but by instituting separability, adjacency, and lawful passage.
